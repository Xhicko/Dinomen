FAQs

HOW CAN I BUY DINOGENTS?

Dinogents purchase happens through our website page.
When minting commences, login in into your computer, connect your wallet and hit the mint Button, upon confirmation of your transaction, your Dinogents will be sent to your address.
PS: minting will be limited to only one (1) transaction at a time, therefore you can mint as many times as you want.

WHEN CAN WE START MINTING?

We will be announcing a launch date as soon as possible. We've seen part of NFT launches that go south and we're wary of the frustrations that come with it. We want to make sure everything is perfect and that everything runs smoothly to provide you the very best experience

WILL THERE BE A SECONDARY MARKET FOR DINOGENTS?

Yes we will be listing on SolanartNFT marketplace as soon as the minting process is completed so you can trade your Dinogents, we are also in contact with Digital Eyes, and therefore, there will be marketplaces for your Dinogents.

WILL THERE BE SECONDARY MARKET ROYALTIES?

Yes, Royalties are set at 4.5%. 10% of the royalties will also be contributed back to the community funds for future development.

WHY SOLANA?

We think Solana technology is amazing for NFT's and we want to be part of the movement. We were very attracted to the idea of low gas fees not just for minting but also for the secondary market and also the faster to confirm all transaction on the network.

CAN I MINT ON MY MOBILE?

Yes, you will be able to mint through Sollet on your Mobile, to mint through your computer; you have to connect your phantom.

DO YOU HAVE A RARITY SYSTEM?

Yes, the rarities of the Dinogents is of great essence to us, therefore we have our rarities ranging from Legendary, Super rare, Rare, Uncommon and common Dinogents with corresponding percentages % or chances of being minted. View rarity tables: click here

WILL THERE BE A COMMUNITY FUNDS?

Yes, 30% of our total revenue generated from the sales and Secondary market Royalties will all be kept in our community Vault for future project development, this will enable us hire the right contractors and developers to build and ensure the sustainability of our project.

WHAT IS THE MINTING PRICE?

We will mint at 2SOL and 4SOL respectively, we will have two mint buttons, this implies that you can opt for 2SOL minting or 4SOL minting depending on the rarity of Dinogents you wish to get.
Furthermore, you will have a greater chance of getting a legendary and super rare when you hit the 4SOL mint button, while the other rarities falls in the 2SOL mint session

WHAT IS THE SUPPY CAP?

The Supply for the first minting is only 1,500 arts for all categories. If requested by the members of the community, we will listen to them and can launch the next batch as a support to satisfy all members of our community for the beautiful arts we are producing.
